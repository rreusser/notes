    B.

    Parameters
    ----------
    uplo: string
        specifies whether the upper or lower triangular part is referenced.

        number of columns.

    IPIV: Int32Array
        `IPIV`.

    strideIPIV: integer
        stride length for `IPIV`.

    B: Float64Array

        leading dimension of `B`.
