    ----------

    A: Float64Array
        `A`.

    LDA: integer

    IPIV: Int32Array
        `IPIV`.

    strideIPIV: integer
        stride length for `IPIV`.

    B: Float64Array
        `B`.

    LDB: integer
        leading dimension of `B`.
