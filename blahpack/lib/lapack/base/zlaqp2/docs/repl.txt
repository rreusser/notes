    Computes a QR factorization with column pivoting of the M-by-N matrix A.

    Parameters
    ----------
    order: string
        Row-major (C-style) or column-major (Fortran-style) order.

    M: integer
        number of rows.

    N: integer
        number of columns.

    offset: integer
        `offset`.

    A: Float64Array
        `A`.

    LDA: integer
        leading dimension of `A`.

    JPVT: Float64Array
        `JPVT`.

    strideJPVT: integer
        stride length for `JPVT`.

    TAU: Float64Array
        `TAU`.

    strideTAU: integer
        stride length for `TAU`.

    VN1: Float64Array
        `VN1`.

    strideVN1: integer
        stride of dimension 1 of `VN`.

    VN2: Float64Array
        `VN2`.

    strideVN2: integer
        stride of dimension 2 of `VN`.

    WORK: Float64Array
        `WORK`.

    strideWORK: integer
        stride length for `WORK`.

    out: Float64Array
